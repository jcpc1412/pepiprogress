# 01 — Positioning & Strategy

## Who it's for
People running peptide / GLP-1 / wellness-compound protocols who want to *prove to themselves it's working* — and who currently rely on spreadsheets, Notes, and inconsistent mirror selfies.

Primary goals users come with (multi-select): weight loss, skin/appearance, recovery, sleep, body composition, general wellness.

## The two products
1. **Personal tracker** — immediate, day-one value. Must be excellent *with zero other users*.
2. **Community outcomes DB** — the long-term moat. Worthless empty, so it's earned over time from (1).

> Strategic risk: a community-data product that needs users to be valuable. MVP must not depend on it.

## Competitive landscape
Crowded on logging; open on our wedge.

| App | Strength | Gap we exploit |
|-----|----------|----------------|
| PeptIQ | Education + calculators, multi-platform | No photo-consistency, no community outcomes |
| Peptify | Vials/bloodwork/protocols | Same |
| PepTracker / Pep Planner | Scheduling, syringe math | Same |
| Regimen | Complex multi-compound stacks | Same |
| Pep AI / Peptide AI | "AI" branding, GLP-1 focus | AI is light; no vision/photo analysis |

**Our wedge:** (a) AI-assisted *photo consistency + visual progress*, (b) *community outcomes* with provenance — what actually happened for people with stacks/goals like yours (dosing *guidance* deferred until legally sound; we lead with outcomes, not doses), (c) a *dynamic integrations* layer pulling from Health/fitness/scales so logging is mostly automatic.

## Positioning statement
> For people on peptide protocols who can't tell if it's actually working, PepiProgress is a progress tracker that makes your photos and metrics genuinely comparable over time and shows you what the community's outcomes say — unlike dose-logging apps that just record what you took.

## Success metrics (draft)
- Activation: % who complete onboarding → first log → first photo within 48h.
- Habit: D7 / D30 logging retention.
- Wedge proof: % of users with ≥2 comparable photo sessions; photo-quality score trend.
- Data flywheel: % of logs auto-filled via integrations; community records contributed per active user.

## Decisions (locked)
- **Launch scope: broad peptide from day one.** All goals/peptides supported at launch rather than niching to GLP-1 first. We compete on the wedge (photo consistency + community + integrations), not on narrowing the audience.
- **Community DB is opt-in** (off by default), separate from core use. The tracker is fully functional without contributing; opting in unlocks community insights in return. Required by GDPR/BIPA for health+biometric data (see 11).
